Server Rules

Read carefully to keep Los Santos RP immersive and fair. Breaking rules may result in sanctions.

Read Rules Appeals

Core Rules

  1. 1
    No Metagaming or RDM

    Do not use OOC information to affect IC actions. Random Deathmatch (killing without RP reason) is prohibited unless in permitted scenarios.

  2. 2
    Respect Staff & Players

    Harassment, discrimination, or aggressive behavior will not be tolerated. Follow staff instructions and appeal politely if needed.

  3. 3
    IC Economy and Exploits

    Exploiting glitches or duping items/money is forbidden. Report exploits to staff immediately.

  4. 4
    Whitelist & Roleplay Standards

    Follow whitelist application guidelines; keep characters believable and consistent with server lore and rules.

  5. 5
    Cheats and Third-Party Tools

    Use of cheats, hacks, or unauthorized tools is a permanent ban offense.

How to Join

If you have questions, join our Discord for clarifications and appeals.
